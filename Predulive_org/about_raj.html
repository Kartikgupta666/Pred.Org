About US

Heliware is a 3D Geo-Spatial analytics platform.

Who are You

A Web-GL Powered Geo-Spatial Analytics Platform for developers, analytics & data scientists which provides tools for GIS, Web Mapping and spatial data science & helps companies to get insight in just a few clicks. Heliware was founded by Rajan Srivastav who is a leading entrepreneur with passion, ethics, vision and hard work in 2018.

What We Are

Heliware is a leading Geo-spatial start-up in India which provides analytics, modeling, simulation and visualization of spatial data. Heliware has its own tech stack (library & API) being used by 15k developers around the globe. We provide customized software and services to defense, solar, construction, urban planning and several other industries.

Our Mission

Making “No Code” geospatial tech-stack, which helps customer to get the best solutions and its deliverables.
